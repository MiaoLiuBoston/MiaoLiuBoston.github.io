Hypothesis Discovery with AI Research Agents
in Finance and Accounting

Read the Paper

Project Overview

This project develops and evaluates AI research agents capable of autonomously generating and evaluating novel empirical hypotheses in finance and accounting. By combining large language models with structured financial data and domain-specific reasoning, these agents can propose, refine, and rank research conjectures — compressing early-stage idea generation and enabling researchers to explore a broader landscape of questions.

Key Themes

Agentic Hypothesis Generation

AI agents that autonomously explore financial datasets and propose theory-grounded empirical hypotheses, with supporting rationale and testability assessments.

Finance & Accounting Domain

Grounded in the institutional context of corporate disclosure, capital markets, and financial reporting — where structured and textual data are abundant.

Human–AI Collaboration

Examines how researchers interact with AI-generated hypotheses — when they adopt, refine, or reject them — to understand human judgment in AI-assisted discovery.
